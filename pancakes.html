The recipe for some delicious Pancakes is as follows!

You will need the following ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Sugar 2 tablespoons
Baking powder 1 tablespoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Eggs 2 large
Milk 1 3/4 cups
Unsalted butter, melted 1/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon

Instructions:

  • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • In another bowl, beat the eggs, then add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. The batter may have some lumps; that's okay.
  • Preheat a griddle or skillet over medium-high heat. Lightly grease with butter or oil.
  • For each pancake, pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle.
  • Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip the pancake and cook the other side until golden brown.
  • Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.
