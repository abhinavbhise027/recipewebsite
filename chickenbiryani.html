The recipe for some delicious Chicken biryani is as follows!

You will need the following ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Basmati rice 2 cups
Chicken (bone-in, cut into pieces) 500 grams
Onions (sliced) 2 large
Tomatoes (chopped) 2 medium
Yogurt 1/2 cup
Ginger paste 1 tablespoon
Garlic paste 1 tablespoon
Biryani masala 2 teaspoons
Ghee 2 tablespoons
Oil 2 tablespoons
Whole spices (bay leaves, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom) As needed
Mint leaves A handful
Cilantro (coriander leaves) A handful
Saffron strands (soaked in milk) A pinch
Warm milk 1/4 cup
Salt To taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee and oil. Add the whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add the ginger and garlic paste, and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  6. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink on the outside.
  7. Add the biryani masala, yogurt, mint leaves, and cilantro. Mix well.
  8. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil and cook the soaked rice until it's 70% cooked. Drain the rice.
  9. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture in the pot.
  10. Sprinkle saffron-soaked milk, fried onions, mint leaves, and cilantro over the rice.
  11. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, allowing the rice and chicken to cook through and absorb the flavors.
  12. Turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before gently fluffing the rice with a fork.
  13. Serve the chicken biryani hot with raita or salad on the side.
