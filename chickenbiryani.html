The recipe for some delicious Chicken biryani is as follows!

You will need the following ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Basmati rice 2 cups
Chicken (bone-in, cut into pieces) 500 grams
Onions (sliced) 2 large
Tomatoes (chopped) 2 medium
Yogurt 1/2 cup
Ginger paste 1 tablespoon
Garlic paste 1 tablespoon
Biryani masala 2 teaspoons
Ghee 2 tablespoons
Oil 2 tablespoons
Whole spices (bay leaves, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom) As needed
Mint leaves A handful
Cilantro (coriander leaves) A handful
Saffron strands (soaked in milk) A pinch
Warm milk 1/4 cup
Salt To taste

Instructions:

  • Wash the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  • In a large pot, heat ghee and oil. Add the whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add the sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add the ginger and garlic paste, and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink on the outside.
  • Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, allowing the rice and chicken to cook through and absorb the flavors.
  • Turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before gently fluffing the rice with a fork.
